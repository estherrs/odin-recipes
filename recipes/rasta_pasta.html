Rasta Pasta

Description

This dish is Jamaican inspired with the 3 colored bell peppers and the jerk seasoning, itâ€™s a very comforting pasta dish that has all the cream and cheese that you need to feel good. It also has a strong flavor, and a lovely spicy kick that will make you want to have this pasta all the time!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 1 pound of penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup of green onions
  • 3 tbsp of jerk seasoning
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable Stock

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted water.
  2. Slice the bell peppers, green onions, and garlic.
  3. Add vegetable oil to the pot and saute the bell peppers with the green onions. Add the minced garlic and cook until it's fragrant.
  4. Add jerk seasoning to the pot.
  5. Add heavy cream and vegetable stock to te pot. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. Add parmesan cheese to the pot, and stir until it melts in the sauce.
  7. Add the chicken pieces and pasta and give everything a good mix so the ingredients are well coated in the sauce.
  8. Garnish with chopped chives or parsley and serve.
